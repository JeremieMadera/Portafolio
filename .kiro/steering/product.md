# Product Overview

Personal portfolio website for Jeremie Madera, a back-end developer showcasing skills, projects, and contact information.

## Key Sections
- Home: Introduction and social links
- About Me (Sobre Mi): Personal background and education
- Projects (Proyectos): Project showcase with technology details
- Skills (Habilidades): Technical skills with visual icons
- Contact (Contacto): Footer with social media links

## Language
Primary language is Spanish (lang="es"). All content, navigation, and labels should be in Spanish.

## Target Audience
Potential employers, recruiters, and professional contacts looking to learn about the developer's background and capabilities.
